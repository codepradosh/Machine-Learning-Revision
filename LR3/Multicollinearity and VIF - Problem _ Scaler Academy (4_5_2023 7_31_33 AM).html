VIF of 1 represents no multicollinearity
VIF of 0 represents no multicollinearity
A VIF of 4 indicates that multicollinearity inflates the variance by a factor of 4 compared to a model with no multicollinearity
If a set of independent variables explains significant amount of variance of remaining independent variable, it represents multicollinearity.
